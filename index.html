Why Xolentum?

Most of the existing cryptocurrencies, like Bitcoin and Ethereum, have transparent blockchains. This means that your activity and transactions are traceable by anyone. With Xolentum that's not the case. Xolentum transactions are untraceable, since the sending, receiving addresses as well as transacted amounts remain private.

Xolentum uses the RandomX hashing algorithm, which means that it is ASIC resistant, and mineable only by CPU and GPU. Our main objective behind this, is that the person mining even on the minimal hardware should not go unrewarded.

Xolentum has a 30-second block time, which means that not only transactions are secure, but fast too. Moreover, the network fee is very low.
